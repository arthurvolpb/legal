Privacy Policy — Rocks & Pines Automator

This software is a personal automation tool operated by Rocks & Pines Art.

Data collected

The application accesses the operator's own Pinterest Business account through OAuth solely to create Pins on boards owned by the operator. The application does not collect, store, track, or share data about Pinterest users, viewers, followers, or any third party.

Data storage

OAuth tokens are stored locally on the operator's machine only. The application does not store data on external servers.

Third-party services

The application only communicates with the official APIs required for its automation workflow, including Pinterest, Meta, Etsy, Google Gemini, and Serper. No data is sold, shared, or transferred to third-party analytics or tracking services.

Data sharing

None. The application does not use analytics, advertising pixels, tracking tools, or third-party data recipients.
